About Us

We all know how difficult it can be to pick a meal plan and sticking to it when you wanna get in shape. Food For Days is an easy to use tool to help you keep track of your macros and to pick a meal plan that works best for you. Users can search out database and find their favorite foods and their favorite recipes with all the nutritional information attached. You can save them for later and use them to make the perfect meal plan for you. Food For Days is excited to help you achieve all your dietary goals and needs.
